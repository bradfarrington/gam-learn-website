What we do at GamLEARN

At GamLEARN we support people facing the criminal justice system due to gambling and provide support for those impacted around them. We provide training to organisations to recognise gambling harms, work with researchers to drive change and empower people with lived experience through events, research and connection.

Supporting People Within the Criminal Justice System

Our work extends to supporting people affected by gambling harm within the criminal justice system. We provide a voice for lived experience in conversations around policy, rehabilitation, and recovery.

What kind of support does GamLEARN offer?

We provide access to peer-led recovery groups, one-to-one support, awareness workshops, and professional training. Our aim is to create safe, empowering spaces for people impacted by gambling harm to connect and rebuild.
