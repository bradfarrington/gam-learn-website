Words That Show
The Journey

Words That Show
The Journey

At the start of our CJS meetings, we invite everyone to check in by sharing a single word that describes how they’re feeling in that moment. We close the sessions the same way, asking for one word that reflects how they feel after our time together.


This simple practice creates a safe, supportive space to express emotions, and we are proud to say that most people leave feeling supported, hopeful, and uplifted, surrounded by others who understand and share similar experiences.

What kind of support does GamLEARN offer?

We provide access to peer-led recovery groups, one-to-one support, awareness workshops, and professional training. Our aim is to create safe, empowering spaces for people impacted by gambling harm to connect and rebuild.
